  Deadline for submitting application:
  Voter registration and updating of voter
  records is closed during the 3 months prior 
  to election in Ethiopia

  Read & fill out the form carefully.
					
To register to vote in Ethiopia you must:
- Be a citizen of Ethiopia
- Live in  Ethiopia
- Be at least 18 years of age on or
 before election day.
- Not have been convicted, you must have
 had your civil rights restored.
